## System Specifications

- **Processor:** AMD Ryzen 7 5800X

- **RAM:** 32GB DDR4 3600MHz

- **Model:** ASUS ROG Strix B550-F

- **Features:** Wi-Fi 6, 2.5Gb Ethernet

- **CPU Cooler:** Noctua NH-D15
